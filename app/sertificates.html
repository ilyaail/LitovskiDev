In Insight Out escape room your presentee can choose one of 3 different difficulty level rooms: Poltergeist, Race against Time and The Italian Job. We promise no padlocks in any of them :)

Poltergeist is the light horror escape room with medium difficulty, best for beginners and people who are looking for some jumpscares. Team mission will be to calm down a mad spirit in a haunted house.

Race against Time will be interesting for sci-fi lovers and people who have already been to some escape rooms previously. Here time-traveling and saving the world will be team’s mission.

The Italian Job will be the best for ones loving to beat their brains out. The newest and the most difficult room among all Insight Out rooms. Here the team will become a group of thieves breaking into collectioner’s mansion to steal some worth art object.

It doesn’t matter, which room to choose, best impressions and a lot of fun is guaranteed.
