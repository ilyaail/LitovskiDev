About

Three high quality entertainment and escape room companies - Insight Out, Don’t Panic! and SecretRoom Vilnius cooperated to make it easier for you to choose a gift for your family, friends or colleagues.

You can choose among gift certificates for specific company, which will be valid for any escape room or entertainment of this particular company, or you can choose combined gift certificate that will allow to choose any of 7 entertainments from all 3 companies. Our gift certificates make your present more rememberable, cause impressions from adventure will stay with presentee for a long time!
