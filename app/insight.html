Insight Out gift certificate

This escape room project was created in March 2017 and within 1 year became the #1 escape room in Vilnius due to TripAdvisor reviews from players all over the world.

Insight Out main policy is: “no padlocks in our escape rooms. Electronic and mechanical devices are our base”.

Insight Out gift certificate can be used for one team to play the game in any of Insight Out escape rooms.

There are 3 escape rooms with different levels of difficulty:

Polstergeist (medium), Race against Time (hard) and The Italian Job (very hard)

  • Group size: 2-4 people
  • Duration: 75 min.
  • Location: ŠVilnius, Totorių g. 21 A (in the yard of building 21)
  • Valid for: 6 months.
  • Pre-registration for the game
    is required at:     www.secretroom.lt

And now you need to travel to the past and save the world by performing that experiment in the right way. Will you manage it?

  • Time: 60 min
